    Academic and research staff
      Dr. Arthur Yu ZOU, Professor Dr. Yu ZOU is a professor of Applied Linguistics, Communication University of China (CUC), director of the National Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring & Research Center, a research institution jointly established by Ministry of Education and CUC; he is a research fellow of the State Key Laboratory of Media Convergence & Communication (MCC) at CUC. He is also a head of the Natural Language Processing Research Team at MCC. Currently, his main research interests cover Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring and Research, Corpus Linguistics, and Media Discourse Analysis, etc. More>>>     Dr. Nanchang CHENG, Associate Professor Dr. Nanchang CHENG is an associate professor of National Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring and Research Center at Communication University of China (CUC). He received a PhD in Computational Linguistics in July 2013 from CUC, was a postdoc 2013-2015 at Institute of Automation，Chinese Academy of Sciences, and was on the faculty of CUC since 2015. Currently, his main research interests cover natural language processing and its application to the social media and the public opinion monitoring on web. He also has a course in Computational Linguistics for graduate student.     Dr. Shufeng LIU, Professor Dr. Shufeng LIU was born in March 1978 in Jinan, Shandong Province. Professor of editor by the Academic Journal Center of Communication University of China, deputy editor in chief of China Radio and Television Yearbook. Research Fellow of National Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring & Research Center. He is a Postgraduate Tutor of Communication University of China. He is mainly engaged in the research of Journalism and communication, Compilation and Publication of annals, Cultural industry, etc. He has published more than 20 academic papers in domestic and foreign academic journals such as Communication and Society, Modern Communication and China Publishing. He is the sole author and co editor in chief of 3 academic works, and works as the deputy editor in chief of Beijing chronicles · Radio and Television Records (1994-2010). He has undertaken a number of research projects of the Ministry of education and relevant departments of Beijing Municipality, and participated in several major projects of National Social Science Fund. The paper has won the second prize of Fang Hanqi award of "journalism and Communication Society Award" of The Chinese Association for History of Journalism and Mass Communication, the second prize of "national Yearbook Academic Thesis Award" of Yearbook Working Committee of China Publishing Association, and the first prize of "commemorating the 80th anniversary of the birth of Chinese people's Broadcasting" by China Federation of Radio and Television Associations.     Dr. Hongdong LIU, Professor Dr. Hongdong LIU is a professor of French Language Department of Shandong University. Supervisor for master students. Associate dean of the College of Foreign Languages of Shandong University. Member of the National Foreign Languages Advisory Board under the Ministry of Education. Director of the European Language and Culture Research Center. He is also a research fellow of the National Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring & Research Center. Mainly engaged in researches related to French Teaching Methodology, Language Policies and Language Planning, and Language and Culture Communication. Presided over several projects supported by National Social Science Foundation, State Language Work Committee, Social & Science Foundation of Ministry of Education, and Shandong Social Science Foundation. Edited and coedited several sets of textbooks. Published 2 works and more than 20 theses and policy consultation reports.     Dr. Weiran ZHANG, Associate Professor Weiran ZHANG obtained her PhD in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics from Peking University and in Chinese from Université de Paris Diderot-Paris 7. She is currently an associate professor at the Communication University of China, working both in the department of Linguistics and in National Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring and Research Center. Her research interest includes Media Discourse Analysis, Language Policy, Lexicology, and International Chinese Language Education.     Dr. Sonia Qin SONG, Associate Professor Sonia Qin SONG is an associate professor of Communication Studies at the State Key Laboratory of Media Convergence and Communication, and a research fellow of National Broadcast Media Language Resources Monitoring & Research Center at Communication University of China. She tends to focus on international communication studies, media and digital culture research, public opinion monitoring and analysis, etc. She has published a monograph Research on Western Post-Truth Issues in the Context of Social Media and translated Popular Music, Digital Technology and Society. She has published a number of academic papers in domestic and International journals, and a number of special reports have been adopted. More>>>        Last update: Aug 31, 2023
